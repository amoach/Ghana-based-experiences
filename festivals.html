ITINERARY

28th - 30th December 2021 Afrochella!
The best way to be welcomed into Ghana, we're you'll get to party like a true Ghanaian as well as being immersed into performances which will give you a little experience of some Ghanaian traditions. Get down all day long with the likes of Ghana's very own hiplife grandfather Daddy Lumba to Stonebwoy to Juls. Josef Adamu will also be curating his highly anticipated international tour of the 'Hair Appoinment' all week. Come to show out and have a makeshift photoshoot and go away with some mementos. Truly is a week of culture and partying!

3rd - 6th January 2022 Afronation!
This week will feel like a non-stop party as not only is there a crazy line up including Wizkid, Burna Boy and Ghana's very own Sarkodie, and so many others! The Ghanaian sun can get draining this time of the year so this location includes pools by the bars, chill and mingle when you're not dancing away. It all takes place on a beach so also feel free to take a dip, no chance of dehydration here! And as if that wasn't all enough to keep the party going, we'll be holding competitions throughout the week -maybe we'll ask you dance, maybe complete a dare, who knows; but you could win prizes such as spa weekends, VIP access all areas tickets and so much more!
